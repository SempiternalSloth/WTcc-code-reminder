Gaijin Store Partner HUD

A simple reminder tool to ensure you're supporting your favorite creators on the Gaijin Store.

Step 1: Download

Download the extension files to your computer and unzip them.

Download Extension (.zip)

Step 2: Installation

Open Extensions: In Chrome, go to chrome://extensions/
Enable Developer Mode: Toggle the switch in the top right corner to ON.
Load Extension: Click "Load unpacked" and select the folder you just unzipped.
